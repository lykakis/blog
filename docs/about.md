# About

I'm Pavlos Lykakis, a software engineer based in Heraklion, Crete.

I work at [Cytech Mobile](https://www.cytechmobile.com/) on [mCore](https://www.cytechmobile.com/mobile/mcore-a2p-wholesale-platform/) and [Sendium](https://github.com/cytechmobile/sendium) — an open-source headless SMS gateway built with Java and Quarkus.

This site is where I write about things I'm learning.

Outside of work I also enjoy photography — see [Displaced](/photos/displaced), a photo essay on the housing crisis in Heraklion.

The idea for this site came after I discovered [Steph Ango's blog](https://stephango.com/). I admire the simplicity of his writing. These three posts stood out to me:

- [Don't delegate understanding](https://stephango.com/understand)
- [Choose optimism](https://stephango.com/optimism)
- [What can we remove?](https://stephango.com/remove)
